# Gemini Operational Mandates

## 1. Do Not Remove
*   **NEVER** remove or revert any code, features, or functionality unless I explicitly ask you to do so. This includes "cleaning up" or "refactoring" that removes logic.

## 2. Strictly Follow TODO
*   **ALWAYS** use `TODO.md` as the primary source of truth for project progress. 
*   Update `TODO.md` immediately after completing a task.
*   Prioritize tasks in the order they appear in `TODO.md` unless I direct you otherwise.

## 3. UI/UX "Masterpiece" Standards
*   **Aura & Glass Philosophy**: ALWAYS adhere to the `UI_GUIDELINES.md` when building or modifying UI. Mavault relies on deep blurs, physical tactility (pressed glass physics), and a monochromatic base.
*   **No Standard HTML Elements**: NEVER use standard, unstyled HTML buttons, inputs, or dropdowns. Every interactive element must feel premium and custom-built.
*   **Bento Box Over Lists**: When displaying complex data, ALWAYS prefer the CSS Grid "Bento Box" layout (`.bento-grid`, `.bento-card`) over linear top-to-bottom lists.
*   **Micro-interactions**: Interactive elements must ALWAYS include `transform: scale()` or translation hover/active states to create a physical feel.
